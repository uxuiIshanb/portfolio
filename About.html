Product Designer, Multidisciplinary Creator,
EV Platform Specialist

I'm Ram, a product designer and illustrator from India, passionate about building digital experiences that are scalable, modern, and user-centric. I graduated in Fashion Design from NIFT (National Institute of Fashion Technology), New Delhi, and have led UI/UX for EV platforms, including white-label solutions for businesses and organizations.

My work spans designing for 1M+ users, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and driving product visions from concept to launch. I thrive in environments that value ethical product development, rapid iteration, and clear communication. Previously, I worked as a product designer with Workruit (Remote, Hyderabad), and contributed to international projects in Paris and with Logicurf.

Skills & Interests

My expertise includes product and visual design, storytelling, prototyping, wireframing, and data visualization. I enjoy tackling complex design challenges, conducting user research, and creating interactive prototypes for web and mobile platforms.

I am passionate about information design, making educational videos, and illustration. Outside of work, I love solo adventures, long rides on highways, and exploring new places. I find energy in creative pursuits and continuous learning.

Experience

Workruit (2021–2024, Remote): Led product design for HR platforms, creating mid- and high-fidelity wireframes, interactive prototypes, and collaborating with global teams.

EV Platform Case Study: Designed scalable, fundable EV solutions for organizations, focusing on usability, investor confidence, and modern UI.

Opera National De Paris (2018): Exchange program, contributed to design research and visual storytelling.

Logicurf (2018, Intern): Designed flyers, brochures, social media graphics, and book layouts.
