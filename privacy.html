1. Scope

This policy explains how I (Ishan Bansal) collect and use personal data when you visit lumas.studio and related pages. It applies only to this site.

2. Data I Collect
  • Technical data (automatic): IP address, device/browser info, operating system, referrer URL, time of access, pages viewed.
  • Usage data: clicks, scrolls, page interactions, session flow (PostHog).
  • Analytics data: aggregated traffic insights (Google Search Console etc.).
  • Voluntary data: information you send by email or contact form.
3. Purpose & Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)
  • Site security & performance (legitimate interests)
  • Analytics to improve content & design (legitimate interests; consent for non‑essential cookies where required)
  • Responding to inquiries (contract / legitimate interests)
  • Legal obligations (where applicable)

I do not sell or rent personal data.

4. Tools & Processors
  • PostHog (analytics & session replays)
  • Google Search Console (aggregate search insights)
  • Hosting/CDN (e.g., Vercel/Netlify) (serving & security)
  • Email (Google Workspace) (inquiry handling)

Each acts as a processor under a DPA where applicable.

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies
  • Essential cookies: security & core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: PostHog & similar measurement.
  • Where legally required (EU/UK), non‑essential cookies load only after consent.

You can control cookies via browser settings.

6. International Transfers

Data may be processed in India, the EU, and the US. For transfers outside the EEA/UK I rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards offered by providers.

7. Retention
  • Server logs: up to 30 days.
  • Analytics (PostHog): typically 12–18 months.
  • Contact / inquiries: up to 24 months.
  • Legal / contract data: up to 7 years.

Data is deleted or anonymized after these periods.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, withdraw consent. Contact: connect@lumas.studio. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

9. US Residents (CPRA / CPA / CTDPA / VCDPA)

I do not sell or share personal information. Request access or deletion via connect@lumas.studio.

10. Children

This site is not directed at children under 16 and I do not knowingly collect such data.

11. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Latest version: lumas.studio/privacy.
